The Hygge Mentality





















Hey there! Welcome to the Hygge family! Hygge (pronounced “Hoo-Gah”) is a danish word that translates to “a feeling of comfort and contentment”. This is exactly what we want our Hygge fam to feel when they visit our website. This is a website created for students by students. School is stressful, and we get that. Hygge is your escape route. A place where you can come to see some funny videos for a good laugh, or find some guided meditations for those moments of anxiety, or find some good study music, or even be redirected to get some real time help if struggling with something more major.

Wondering what the pineapple has to do with all this? Nothing! And that’s the point, Hygge is where you can just let your guard down and not try to make sense of anything. Just relax and enjoy, and be as chill as this pineapple on the beach. That’s the Hygge Mentality, and don’t worry you will catch on fast!
